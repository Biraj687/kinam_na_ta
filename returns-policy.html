Return Eligibility

To ensure a fair marketplace for both buyers and sellers, we maintain strict but clear eligibility criteria for returns.

  • The item must be in the same condition as received: unworn, unused, with all original tags attached.
  • The product must be in its original, undamaged packaging.
  • A valid proof of purchase (digital invoice or physical receipt) is required.
  • The return request must be filed within 7 calendar days of the delivery date.

Non-Returnable Items

For health, hygiene, and safety reasons, certain products cannot be returned or exchanged unless they arrive defective.

Personal Care

Beauty products, perfumes, and intimate apparel.

Custom Goods

Personalized items or products made to order.

Additionally, perishable goods like food or plants, and digital products/software downloads are strictly non-returnable.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged upon arrival. If you need to exchange an item for a different size or color, the fastest method is to return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired variation.

Refunds & Processing

Once our quality assurance team receives and inspects your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • Digital Wallets (eSewa/Khalti): Refunds typically appear in your wallet within 24-48 hours of approval.
  • Bank Transfers: May take 3-5 business days depending on your bank's processing times.
  • Cash on Delivery: For COD orders, we will request your bank details or wallet ID to process the refund digitally.

Quality Guaranteed.

Every product on Kinam Na Ta is inspected for quality. If we made a mistake, we'll make it right—no questions asked.
