3. Shipment Confirmation & Tracking

You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours. You can also track your order directly from your Account Dashboard.

4. Damages & Incorrect Address

Kinam Na Ta is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping if the incorrect address was provided. If you receive your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim or reach out to our support team for assistance.

Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.

5. International Shipping

We currently do not ship outside of Nepal. We are working on expanding our services to international customers in the future.
