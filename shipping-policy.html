Order Processing

We know you're excited to receive your order. That's why we process all orders within 12 to 24 hours of confirmation.

  • Orders placed before 2 PM are typically processed the same business day.
  • Orders placed on Saturdays or public holidays will be processed the following business day.
  • You will receive an automated SMS and email once your order has been handed over to our logistics partner.

Delivery Rates & Timelines

We offer competitive shipping rates tailored to your location. Timelines are estimates and may vary due to weather or road conditions.

Region Timeline Rate
Kathmandu Valley 1 - 2 Days NPR 100
Major Cities (Pokhara, Biratnagar, etc) 3 - 5 Days NPR 150
Remote Regions 5 - 7 Days NPR 250

Note: Free shipping is automatically applied to all orders with a subtotal exceeding NPR 5,000.

Order Tracking

Stay updated on your package's journey. Every Kinam Na Ta order comes with real-time tracking.

Once your order is dispatched, you can track it via:

  • The link provided in your shipping confirmation email.
  • Your account dashboard under "Order History".
  • Directly on our logistics partner's website using your KNT tracking number.

Damage & Loss Insurance

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. We take full responsibility for your order until it is safely in your hands.

  • Damaged Goods: If your product is damaged during transit, please notify us within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement.
  • Lost Packages: If your order does not arrive within the estimated timeline, we will conduct an investigation and issue a full refund or resend the items at no extra cost.
